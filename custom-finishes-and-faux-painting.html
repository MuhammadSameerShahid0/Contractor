What is faux painting?

Faux painting is a decorative technique that replicates materials like marble, wood, or stone using specialized paint applications, adding depth, texture, and elegance to your space.

What custom finishes do you offer?

We offer a wide range of finishes, including marbleizing, antiquing, suede effects, Venetian plaster, textured stone looks, and metallic washes — all tailored to your unique style.

How long does a custom finish project take?

Project timelines depend on the size and complexity, but most custom finishes are completed within a few days to a week. We'll give you a detailed schedule during consultation.

Can you match an existing finish or idea?

Yes! Our artisans specialize in color matching and texture replication. Share your vision or inspiration with us, and we’ll craft a finish that perfectly aligns with your expectations.

Are your materials and paints eco-friendly?

We prioritize health and sustainability by using low-VOC, eco-friendly paints and premium materials that are safe for your family and kind to the environment.

How should I maintain a faux finish or custom texture?

Our finishes are low-maintenance. We recommend light dusting and gentle cleaning. At project handover, we’ll provide simple care tips to keep your walls flawless for years to come.
