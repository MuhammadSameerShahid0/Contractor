How long does exterior painting take?

Most exterior painting jobs take 3–5 days, depending on the size and prep work needed. We’ll give you an accurate timeline during your consultation.

What type of paint do you use for exteriors?

We use high-quality, weather-resistant exterior paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams and Behr, designed to handle the SoCal climate.

Do I need to be home during the painting?

Nope! As long as we have access to the exterior, we can work while you're away — just ensure gates and areas are unlocked.

Is power washing included?

Yes. We always power wash and clean surfaces before painting to ensure long-lasting adhesion and a clean finish.

Can you help me choose the right color?

Definitely. Our team offers expert color consultations to help you find shades that match your home’s architecture and personality.

How do I maintain the new paint job?

Wash occasionally with a hose and mild soap. Avoid pressure washing unless necessary. We also offer touch-up services for long-term maintenance.
