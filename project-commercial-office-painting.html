Commercial Office Painting

Linkedin Twitter Facebook
Client:
Horizon Corporate Group
Date:
April 10, 2025
Category:
Commercial Painting
Project Value:
$7,500.00

Project Overview

This corporate office needed a refresh that aligned with their modern, professional branding. We provided a complete interior repainting service using low-odor, fast-drying paint to minimize disruption to business operations.

The Horizon Corporate Group opted for a sleek, neutral palette to complement their furniture and glass partitions. We worked after hours to avoid interference with daily workflows, completing the job on time and with precision.

Painting Process

We used commercial-grade rollers and sprayers for even application across textured drywall and accent panels. Our team followed safety protocols and ensured surfaces were properly prepped and protected.

  • Low-VOC, eco-friendly paint for safe indoor use.
  • Color consultation to align with corporate branding.
  • Daily progress updates to management.
  • Final walk-through and touch-up service included.
The Challenge

Timing was critical. The client required minimal impact on their 9-to-5 operations. We scheduled crews during off-hours and worked in phases to complete different sections without causing downtime.

Project Completed

The office was fully painted in 4 evenings and 1 weekend, with zero business interruptions. Furniture and tech setups were fully protected and returned to their original positions post-project.

The Result

The new look delivered a polished, modern feel that employees loved and clients complimented. The office now feels more cohesive, energized, and brand-aligned.
