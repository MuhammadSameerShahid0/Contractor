Residential Interior Painting

Linkedin Twitter Facebook
Client:
Emily & James Carter
Date:
February 10, 2025
Category:
Interior Painting
Project Value:
$2,400.00

Project Overview

For this cozy three-bedroom home, we focused on transforming the living spaces into bright, modern, and welcoming environments. The homeowners wanted a minimalist yet warm vibe with satin finishes to enhance lighting and depth.

Each room was personalized with custom tones to match its purpose — from calming neutrals in the bedrooms to a bold accent wall in the living area. The project demanded high attention to edge detailing and clean linework.

Painting Process

Our team prepped each room with precision — covering floors and furniture, repairing wall imperfections, and applying stain-blocking primer. We used low-VOC paints to ensure a safe environment for the family.

  • Color consultation to align with clients’ vision and decor.
  • Wall prep including sanding, patching, and priming.
  • Use of eco-friendly, low-odor Benjamin Moore paints.
  • Detailed brushwork around trims, edges, and fixtures.
The Challenge

The walls had aged, uneven surfaces and multiple nail holes. Plus, the kitchen had grease build-up that required a deeper surface treatment before painting could begin.

Project Completed

Completed in 4 days, we maintained a dust-free, disruption-minimal worksite, allowing the family to remain in their home throughout. Final walk-through included minor touchups and a care guide for maintenance.

The Result

The home's interior now feels brighter, more cohesive, and perfectly aligned with the client's modern taste. They were thrilled with the transformation and left us a glowing 5-star review.
