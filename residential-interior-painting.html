How long does an interior painting project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the size of your home and the number of rooms being painted, but most projects are completed within a few days. We provide an estimated timeline during our consultation to ensure we meet your expectations.

What types of finishes are available?

We offer a variety of finishes, including matte, satin, eggshell, and gloss, as well as specialty finishes for specific needs. Our team will help you choose the best finish for each room, considering lighting and the desired aesthetic.

Do I need to prepare my home before the painting begins?

No need to worry about preparation! Our team handles everything, from covering furniture to prepping surfaces. We ensure your space is protected and ready for the perfect coat of paint.

Can I select my own colors for the project?

Absolutely! We offer color consultations to help you choose the best colors for your home. We’ll work with you to ensure the shades you select complement your décor and personal style.

Are the paints you use eco-friendly?

Yes! We prioritize the use of eco-friendly, low-VOC paints that are safe for you and the environment, providing beautiful results without compromising on health and safety.

How should I maintain my newly painted interior?

Our interior finishes are designed for durability and ease of maintenance. Light dusting and gentle cleaning are usually all that’s needed to keep your walls looking fresh. We’ll provide specific care instructions during the project handover.
