Wallpaper Installation Services in Los Angeles

Elevate your interior spaces with stunning wallpaper designs tailored to your style. At Delosweb, we specialize in professional wallpaper installation that transforms plain walls into statement pieces. Whether you're looking for bold patterns, subtle textures, or custom prints — we've got the tools and talent to bring your vision to life.

Why Choose Our Wallpaper Services?

Our experienced team handles everything from wall prep to precision application, ensuring a smooth, bubble-free finish every time. We work with vinyl, fabric, peel-and-stick, grasscloth, and more — always applying with care and attention to detail.

Not sure what design works best for your space? We offer style consultations to help you pick the perfect pattern, texture, or mural that complements your room's vibe and lighting.

Our Benefits

We don’t just install — we perfect. From removing old wallpaper to treating and priming the wall surface, our team ensures your new wallpaper adheres cleanly and lasts for years. We show up on time, respect your space, and leave you with walls that wow.

Precision Application

Our installers align patterns perfectly, cut edges cleanly, and smooth out every inch for a flawless, high-end look.

No Mess, No Stress

We protect your furniture and floors, clean up after ourselves, and leave your home looking better than ever — without the mess.

How long does wallpaper installation take?

Most rooms can be completed in 1–2 days depending on size and design complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Can I bring my own wallpaper design?

Yes! Whether it's a designer brand or a custom print, we’ll install it with precision and care.

Do you remove old wallpaper?

Absolutely. We handle safe, damage-free removal of old wallpaper before prepping for your new design.

Is wallpaper suitable for bathrooms or kitchens?

Yes, we use moisture-resistant options designed specifically for humid environments like bathrooms and kitchens.

How do I maintain my wallpaper?

Simply dust regularly and clean gently with a soft cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners or scrubbing to preserve the finish.

What types of wallpaper do you install?

We install everything from traditional paste-the-wall paper to modern peel-and-stick and fabric-based designs. Whatever your vibe, we’ve got the skills.
